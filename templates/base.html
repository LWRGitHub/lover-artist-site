Fun Fact

Art used to be an Olympic event!

The Olympic games were not always about muscles and the best athletes. The founder of the Games, Baron Pierre de Coubertin, idealized the idea of the true Olympian being a skilled artist and sportsman. Thanks to this, from 1912 and 1948 medals were awarded for sporting-inspired masterpieces of architecture, music, painting, sculpture and literature.
